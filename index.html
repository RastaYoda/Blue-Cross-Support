What is Blue Cross?

Blue Cross is a charity that has been helping sick, injured, abandoned and homeless pets since 1897. Pets help us in so many ways and they depend entirely on us. That's why at Blue Cross we believe in helping pets because pets help us.

We help thousands of pets in need every month, providing veterinary care, expert behaviour help and find them loving happy homes, as well as advice and education for current or future pet owners and pet bereavement support. We develop lifelong relationships with pets and owners, providing quality care that is accessible and non-judgemental. With your support we can give back to more pets in need.

Our vision is that every pet will enjoy a healthy life in a happy home and we won't rest until we achieve it. But we really need your help. We care for around 40,000 pets every year but we know it's not enough - there are many, many more out there who still desperately need us and we must be there for them.

Pets change lives. We change theirs.

Rehoming

We find homes for unwanted cats, dogs, small pets and horses across the UK and our tailor-made service means we help each pet find the right person for them.

Veterinary

Our animal hospitals and pet care clinics provide free veterinary treatment to sick and injured pets when their owners can't afford private veterinary fees.

Education

The Blue Cross Education Team teaches young people and pet owners about pet welfare, staying safe around dogs and responsible pet ownership.

Pet Bereavement Support

When the love and friendship of a pet are gone, life may suddenly seem very empty. If you have lost, or are facing saying goodbye to, a much loved pet and need somebody to talk to, our Pet Bereavement Support Service is here for you every day.

Thousands of pets in UK are suffering everyday. They have to endure a lot of hardships such as abuse, abandonment and extreme temperatures. By donating to Blue Cross, you can transform their lives by helping to provide life-saving operations, paying for their food or finding loving new homes. Any amount you can give us will help change the lives of thousands of pets across the UK. Every bit matters, only with your help we can make a difference.
